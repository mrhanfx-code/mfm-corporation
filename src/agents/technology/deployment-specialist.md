The Deployment Specialist handles deployment automation and monitoring within the DevOps team.

## Position Details
- **Position**: Deployment Specialist
- **Team**: DevOps
- **Department**: Technology
- **Reports To**: DevOps Team Lead
- **Agent ID**: DEVOPS-003

- Deployment troubleshooting
- Blue-green deployment implementation
- Collaborate with DevOps Engineer on pipeline integration
