## 11. Key Success Factors

1. **Speed of response** — Reply to every DM within 15 minutes. Speed = conversion.
2. **Consistency** — Post every single day for 14 days. No days off.
3. **Value-first content** — Don't just sell; share tips, warnings, and useful info.
4. **Social proof** — Screenshot every positive review and repost it.
5. **Urgency** — "Promo RM 47 hanya untuk 14 hari" — then increase to RM 67.
6. ** Malay language** — All content in Malay. English will alienate your core audience.
7. **Payment ease** — Accept DuitNow, online banking, ShopeePay. Don't make payment hard.
8. **Fast delivery** — Send ebook within 5 minutes. Instant gratification builds trust.

## 12. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Facebook group bans** | Read group rules before posting. Post value, not just ads. Rotate groups. |
| **Low engagement** | Test different hooks. A/B test headlines. Use more emotional language. |
| **Payment fraud** | Use DuitNow/bank transfer (safer than PayPal for MYR). Verify payment before sending. |
| **Ebook sharing/piracy** | Add watermark with buyer's name/email. Accept that some sharing will happen. |
| **Negative comments** | Reply professionally. Turn objections into sales opportunities. |
| **Burnout** | Schedule posts in advance. Use Buffer for FB. Batch-create TikToks. |
