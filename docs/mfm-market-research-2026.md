- Incorporated in Malaysia under Companies Act 2016
- Minimum issued share capital: RM 50,000
- In operation for at least 1 year
- Must have Malaysia Digital (MD) or MSC Malaysia status (or active application)
- Must have AI products/services in one of three layers:
  - **Application Layer** (AI-Enabled Solutions): End-user applications or AI-powered solutions — **MFM fits here**
  - Foundation Layer (Infrastructure)
  - Enabling Layer (Model Development)

- Low-interest loan at up to 4% per annum

- Ongoing until fully utilized
- **Not a grant** — must repay principal
