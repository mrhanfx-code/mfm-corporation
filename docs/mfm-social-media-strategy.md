# MFM Corporation — Social Media Strategy 2026

*Generated: May 24, 2026 | Platforms: LinkedIn, X/Twitter, TikTok | Goal: 10K followers Year 1*

## Executive Summary

MFM Corporation's social media strategy operates on two tracks: **internal brand building** (establishing MFM as Malaysia's leading AI automation authority) and **client service revenue** (managing social media for paying clients using self-improving AI agents). Both tracks leverage the same autonomous content engine: AI-generated images, videos, and copy that continuously improve based on engagement data. Target: **10,000 followers** by Month 12, **3 client accounts** on social media retainer by Month 9.

## 2. Platform Strategy

### 2.1 LinkedIn (Primary)

**Audience:** Malaysian SMEs, startup founders, CTOs, business owners

**Content Mix:**
- **40% Educational** — "How AI automation saved this SME RM 50K/year"
- **30% Thought Leadership** — "The future of multi-agent systems in Malaysian enterprise"
- **20% Behind-the-Scenes** — "Watch MFM's 19 agents coordinate a client project"
- **10% Promotional** — Service offerings, case studies, testimonials

**Posting Frequency:** 1 post/day (weekdays)

**Format Priority:**
1. Text + AI-generated image carousels (highest engagement)
2. Polls (engagement boosters)
3. Document posts (PDF guides)
4. Native video (60–90 seconds)

**Growth Tactics:**
- Comment on 5 relevant posts/day (Malaysian business groups)
- Connect with 10 target prospects/week
- Publish 1 long-form article/month on LinkedIn Newsletter
- Join and contribute to 3 Malaysian business groups

### 2.2 X/Twitter (Secondary)

**Audience:** Global AI/tech community, developers, investors

**Content Mix:**
- **50% Technical Insights** — Agent architecture, automation tips, code snippets
- **30% Industry Commentary** — AI news, market trends, competitor analysis
- **20% Company Updates** — Product launches, milestones, team growth

**Posting Frequency:** 2–3 posts/day

**Format Priority:**
1. Threads (3–7 tweets) on AI automation topics
2. Single tweets with AI-generated visuals
3. Quote tweets of industry leaders with MFM perspective

**Growth Tactics:**
- Engage with AI/tech influencers daily (reply with value)
- Use trending hashtags: #AI #Automation #MalaysiaTech #BuildInPublic
- Participate in 2 Twitter Spaces/week on AI topics
- Cross-post LinkedIn content adapted for X format

### 2.3 TikTok (Emerging)

**Audience:** Younger entrepreneurs, tech-curious SMB owners, content creators

**Content Mix:**
- **60% Educational Shorts** — "AI explained in 60 seconds"
- **30% Behind-the-Scenes** — "Day in the life of an AI corporation"
- **10% Trending Audio** — MFM content on trending sounds

**Posting Frequency:** 3–5 videos/week

**Format Priority:**
1. Talking head + AI-generated background visuals
2. Screen recordings with captions (agent demos)
3. AI-generated explainer animations

**Growth Tactics:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds ("This AI saved a Malaysian SME RM 50K...")
- Use Malay + English bilingual captions
- Post at 8pm–10pm MYT (peak Malaysian TikTok usage)
- Stitch/duet trending business content

## 4. Autonomous Posting & Engagement

### 4.1 Self-Optimizing Post Schedule

**How it works:**
1. **Data Collection:** Agent tracks engagement per post by time, day, format, topic
2. **Pattern Recognition:** Analyst agent identifies optimal posting windows per platform
3. **Schedule Adjustment:** Scheduler agent shifts posting times to maximize engagement
4. **A/B Testing:** Test 2 headline variants for each post; winning variant becomes template

**Current Baseline (to be optimized):**
- LinkedIn: 8:30am–9:30am MYT (commute/breakfast)
- X: 12pm–1pm MYT (lunch), 8pm–10pm MYT (evening)
- TikTok: 7pm–10pm MYT (peak leisure time)

### 4.2 Autonomous Engagement

**Comment Reply Agent:**
- Monitors all platforms every 15 minutes
- Generates contextual replies using brand voice
- Escalates sensitive/negative comments to human review
- Learns from high-engagement reply patterns

**DM Response Agent:**
- Auto-responds to common inquiries (pricing, services, booking)
- Qualifies leads (budget, timeline, needs)
- Schedules discovery calls via Calendly integration
- Hands off complex queries to human

**Community Management:**
- Identifies brand mentions across platforms
- Engages with relevant industry conversations
- Follows back engaged accounts in target segment
- Removes spam/bot interactions

## 5. Content QA System

### 5.1 Copy Reviewer Agent Workflow

```
Content Created → Copy Reviewer Scores (0-100)
    ↓
Score ≥ 85: Auto-approve → Schedule
Score 70-84: Approve with minor edits → Human review → Schedule
Score 50-69: Reject with feedback → Copywriter rewrites → Re-review
Score < 50: Escalate to CMO → Full rewrite → Re-review
```

### 5.2 Review Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What Checks |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Brand Voice | 25% | Matches MFM tone (professional, data-driven, confident) |
| Grammar & Spelling | 20% | Zero errors, proper punctuation |
| Persuasiveness | 20% | Clear CTA, emotional hook, logical flow |
| Platform Fit | 15% | Right length, hashtags, format for platform |
| Fact Accuracy | 15% | All claims sourced, no hallucinated data |
| Visual Match | 5% | Image/video aligns with copy message |

### 5.3 Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Professional but accessible. Data-driven but human. Confident but not arrogant.

**Do:**
- Use specific numbers ("43.57% CAGR" not "rapid growth")
- Speak to outcomes ("saved RM 50K" not "improved efficiency")
- Include Malaysia context ("Malaysian SMEs" not just "businesses")
- Use active voice

**Don't:**
- Use hype words ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Make unverifiable claims
- Speak down to audience
- Use excessive jargon without explanation

## 8. Content Improvement Loop

### 8.1 How MFM Content Self-Improves

1. **Publish:** Content goes live across platforms
2. **Measure:** Analyst agent collects engagement data (likes, shares, comments, clicks, saves)
3. **Analyze:** Compare performance vs. historical baseline and vs. A/B test variants
4. **Learn:** Identify patterns ("carousels with 5 slides outperform 10," "Malay-English bilingual captions get 2x engagement")
5. **Adapt:** Update content templates, posting schedule, and visual style guidelines
6. **Generate:** Next batch of content incorporates learnings
7. **Repeat:** Cycle continues, compound improvement over time

### 8.2 Quarterly Content Audit

| Review Item | Action | Owner |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| Top 10 performing posts | Analyze common elements, create templates | trend-spotter |
| Bottom 10 performing posts | Identify failures, update guidelines | quality-reviewer |
| Platform algorithm changes | Adjust strategy, test new formats | trend-spotter |
| Competitor content analysis | Identify gaps, differentiation opportunities | market-analyst |
| Brand voice refresh | Update tone guidelines based on feedback | content-writer |
| Visual style evolution | Update design system based on engagement | idea-generator |
