## Executive Summary

MFM Corporation is a Malaysia-based AI automation consultancy providing enterprise-grade agent frameworks for business operations. Our mission: enable Malaysian SMEs to achieve operational excellence through AI-driven automation at zero infrastructure cost.

**Core Value Proposition**: 42 specialized AI agents, 20 integrated tools, 24/7 autonomous operation, dashboard-first management, zero-cost cloud infrastructure.

**Target Market**: Malaysian SMEs (Small-to-Medium Enterprises) seeking operational efficiency without enterprise IT budgets.

**Revenue Goal**: MYR 80,000/month by Q4 2026.

## Vision

To become Malaysia's leading AI automation consultancy, democratizing enterprise-grade AI capabilities for SMEs through zero-cost infrastructure and premium dashboard experiences.

## Technology Strategy

### Infrastructure
- **Edge Computing**: Cloudflare Workers (global distribution)
- **Database**: D1 SQLite (zero-cost, edge-native)
- **Storage**: R2 (object storage, S3-compatible)
- **Cache**: KV (key-value store, edge caching)
- **Email**: SendGrid (transactional email)
- **Communication**: Telegram Bot API, Slack, SMS (Twilio)

### AI Models
- **Primary**: Cerebras Fast (cost-effective, high performance)

## Risk Management

### Key Risks
1. **Market Adoption**: SMEs may be hesitant about AI automation
   - **Mitigation**: Free trials, case studies, ROI demonstrations

2. **Infrastructure Costs**: Cloudflare pricing changes
   - **Mitigation**: Multi-cloud strategy, cost monitoring, client pass-through

3. **Compliance**: PDPA Malaysia, regional regulations
   - **Mitigation**: Legal counsel, regular audits, compliance frameworks

4. **Competition**: Enterprise solutions entering SME market
   - **Mitigation**: Zero-cost advantage, Malaysia-specific intelligence, premium UX

5. **Technical Debt**: Agent complexity, maintenance burden
   - **Mitigation**: TDD enforcement, systematic debugging, modular architecture
